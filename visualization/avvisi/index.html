How to read the visualization

The visualization shows the number of alerts, references, notes and images per entry.
For each subject, entries with more than 350 daily visits are shown.

The red rectangle represents the alerts, the blue rectangle represents the bibliographical references, the green rectangle represents the notes and the pink rectangle represents the images. The dotted line represents the number of alerts and the total number of references, notes and images in the previous year.

Choose a subject and display order from the menu above for more information.
